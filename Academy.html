⚙️ Smart Assist System

Smart Assist ON

When enabled, AI supports your gameplay by adjusting passes, movement, and actions. This reduces mistakes and helps new players understand flow of the game.

Smart Assist OFF

Disabling Smart Assist gives full manual control over passing, shooting, and player movement. This allows more precision but requires skill.

Real Gameplay Advice

Use Smart Assist ON when learning. Once comfortable, turn it OFF to gain full control, especially for competitive matches and advanced play.

🎮 Controls & Movement

Movement Control

Smooth joystick movement is key. Avoid sharp random movements as they reduce control. Keep your player balanced before making actions.

Sprint Usage

Sprint should be used in open space. Overusing sprint makes you lose control and gives defenders an advantage.

Shielding

Shielding protects the ball from defenders. Use it when under pressure to maintain possession and wait for support.

⚽ Ball Control

First Touch

Your first touch determines your next action. A bad first touch can lead to losing possession. Always control before passing or shooting.

Body Positioning

Face the correct direction before making passes or shots. Poor positioning leads to weak actions.

❌ Beginner Mistakes

- Sprinting too much
- Rushing attacks
- Ignoring positioning
- Making panic passes under pressure

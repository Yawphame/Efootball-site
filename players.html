🧤 Goalkeeper Playstyles

Offensive Goalkeeper

Comes out of the box to clear danger.

👉 Good for high defensive line

Defensive Goalkeeper

Stays close to goal and reacts to shots.

👉 Best for safe and stable defense

---

🛡 Defenders

Build Up

Stays back and helps in passing from defense.

👉 Good for possession play

Destroyer

Aggressive defender that presses and wins the ball.

👉 Use to stop strong attackers

Extra Frontman

Moves forward to support attack.

👉 Risky but useful in attack

---

⚙️ Midfielders

Box-to-Box

Covers attack and defense all game.

👉 Balanced and active player

Anchor Man

Stays back and protects defense.

👉 Best defensive midfielder

Orchestrator

Controls tempo and passes the ball around.

👉 Good for possession style

Hole Player

Makes late runs into the box.

👉 Great for scoring from midfield

Creative Playmaker

Stays central and creates chances.

👉 Best for attacking play

---

⚡ Attackers

Goal Poacher

Always runs behind defenders.

👉 Best for through balls

Deep Lying Forward

Drops back to help build play.

👉 Good for passing attack

Fox in the Box

Stays inside the box waiting for chances.

👉 Best for tap-ins

Target Man

Strong player that holds the ball.

👉 Use for crosses and long balls

Roaming Flank

Cuts inside instead of staying wide.

👉 Good for scoring wingers

Prolific Winger

Stays wide and creates chances.

👉 Best for crossing

⚡ Dribbling Skills (Mobile)

Double Touch
1. Stop running
2. Tap DASH once
3. Swipe analogue left or right quickly

👉 Use in 1v1 situations

Marseille Turn
1. Stop sprint
2. Rotate analogue half circle

👉 Use when surrounded

Step Over
1. Move forward slowly
2. Swipe analogue forward then sideways

👉 Use to confuse defender

⚡ Advanced Mechanics

Acceleration Burst
1. Stop running
2. Tap DASH twice quickly
3. Push analogue forward

👉 Use after skill move

Momentum Dribbling
1. Hold DASH
2. Move analogue smoothly forward

👉 Use in open space

Blitz Curl
1. Face goal from outside box
2. Hold SHOOT
3. Swipe to curve direction

👉 Use for long range curl goals

🎯 Shooting

Stunning Shot
1. Hold SHOOT longer
2. Aim with analogue

👉 Use outside box

First Time Shot
1. Don’t control ball
2. Tap SHOOT instantly

👉 Use for quick attacks

🕹 Dribbling Skills

Double Touch

📱 Mobile: Flick analog left/right quickly while moving

🎮 Console: Move right stick left/right

🧠 When to use: 1v1 situations to beat defenders quickly

Marseille Turn

📱 Mobile: Rotate analog in half circle

🎮 Console: Rotate right stick 180°

🧠 When to use: Tight spaces under pressure

Step Over

📱 Mobile: Quick flick forward then sideways

🎮 Console: Rotate right stick forward

🧠 When to use: To fake direction and create space

---

⚡ Advanced Mechanics

Momentum Dribbling

📱 Mobile: Sprint + move analog smoothly

🎮 Console: Hold sprint + left stick control

🧠 Use: Exploit speed advantage and break defensive lines

Acceleration Burst

📱 Mobile: Tap sprint twice

🎮 Console: Double tap sprint

🧠 Use: Quick escape after skill move

Blitz Curl

📱 Mobile: Shoot + curve swipe

🎮 Console: Shoot + finesse shot

🧠 Use: Edge of box curl shots

---

🎯 Shooting Techniques

Stunning Shot

📱 Mobile: Hold shoot longer

🎮 Console: Hold shoot button

🧠 Use: Long range power shots

First Time Shot

📱 Mobile: Tap shoot instantly when ball comes

🎮 Console: Press shoot immediately

🧠 Use: Fast attacks, no time to control ball

🧠 Pro Tips

✔ Don’t spam skills — use them smartly

✔ Always combine skill + movement

✔ Know when to pass instead of dribble

✔ Practice in training before matches

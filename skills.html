🕹️ Dribbling Skills

Double Touch

👉 Quickly flick joystick left/right while moving

✅ Use when 1v1 vs defender

🔥 Pro Tip: Combine with sprint burst after

Marseille Turn

👉 Rotate joystick in half circle

✅ Use when surrounded or tight space

🔥 Pro Tip: Don’t spam — use once to escape

Ball Roll

👉 Light drag left or right

✅ Use to reposition before pass or shot

🔥 Pro Tip: Perfect for creating shooting angles

🎯 Shooting Techniques

Stunning Shot

👉 Hold shoot button longer

✅ Use outside the box

🔥 Pro Tip: Needs space or it gets blocked

Finesse Shot

👉 Tap shoot lightly + direction

✅ Use inside box or angle

🔥 Pro Tip: Aim far corner always

First-Time Shot

👉 Shoot immediately after pass

✅ Use for fast attacks

🔥 Pro Tip: Don’t control ball first

🎯 Passing Mastery

Through Pass

👉 Press through pass button

✅ Use when striker runs behind defense

🔥 Pro Tip: Timing is everything

One-Touch Pass

👉 Pass instantly after receiving

✅ Use for quick build-up play

🔥 Pro Tip: Avoid holding ball too long

⚡ Advanced Mechanics

Momentum Dribbling

👉 Trigger sprint at correct timing

✅ Use after beating defender

🔥 Pro Tip: Don’t sprint constantly

Acceleration Burst

👉 Tap sprint quickly

✅ Use to create sudden space

🔥 Pro Tip: Best with fast players

Blitz Curl

👉 Special shooting skill (certain players)

✅ Use outside box for curl shots

🔥 Pro Tip: Angle matters more than power

🧬 Player Hub

🔥 Player Roles

Strikers

Responsible for scoring goals. Focus on positioning, finishing, and movement.

Midfielders

Control the game. Distribute passes and connect defense to attack.

Defenders

Stop attacks and protect your goal. Positioning is key.

📊 Player Evaluation

Check speed, dribbling, finishing, and passing before using a player. Each stat affects performance in different situations.

💰 Squad Building

Build a balanced team. Don’t focus only on attack — midfield and defense are equally important.

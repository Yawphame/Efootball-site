🧠 Game IQ

⚔️ Decision Making

Pass or Shoot

If a defender is blocking your path, passing is the better option. If you have space and a clear angle, shooting becomes the correct decision.

Dribble or Pass

Dribble in 1v1 situations to beat defenders. Pass when multiple defenders are around to avoid losing possession.

⏱️ Game Tempo

Slow Build Up

When space is limited, slow the game down and keep possession. This forces the opponent to make mistakes.

Fast Attack

When opponent is out of position, attack quickly before they recover.

📍 Positioning

Good positioning creates opportunities. Always move into space instead of standing behind defenders.

🧠 Reading Opponent

Observe how your opponent defends. If they rush, stay calm. If they sit back, build slowly and create openings.
